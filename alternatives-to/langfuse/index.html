Sipi.bot vs Langfuse

Comparison of Sipi.bot and Langfuse for AI observability and cost control.

Langfuse is a popular open-source LLM observability platform. Sipi.bot focuses on active cost prevention rather than passive monitoring.

If you need deep observability and want to self-host, Langfuse is excellent. If you need to prevent cost overruns before they happen, Sipi.bot's firewall approach is more effective.
