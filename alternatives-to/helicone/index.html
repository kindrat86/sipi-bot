The Best Helicone Alternative in 2026

Looking for a Helicone alternative that enforces spend instead of just observing it? sipi.bot is a pre-spend firewall: per-transaction caps, velocity limits, merchant allowlists, and a human approval queue. Helicone draws you a chart of what your agent spent; sipi.bot blocks the spend before it happens.

Use both. Helicone is excellent at LLM observability — request logs, prompt analytics, cost dashboards. sipi.bot is a pre-spend firewall. They answer different questions and most teams run both. If you only need analytics, keep Helicone. If you need to stop runaway spend, add sipi.bot.

When sipi.bot is the right Helicone alternative

  • Your agent can spend money autonomously and you need to stop runaway spend, not just observe it.
  • You need per-transaction caps, velocity limits, or a merchant allowlist — none of which Helicone offers.
  • You need a compliance-grade audit trail of every spend decision.
  • You need a human-in-the-loop approval queue for large transactions.

When Helicone is still the right tool

  • You need LLM call tracing, prompt analytics, and response quality scoring.
  • Your problem is "understand and optimize my LLM bill," not "stop my agent from overspending."
  • You are OK with observation after the fact.
Bottom line: Helicone tells you what happened. sipi.bot stops what should not happen. Run both if you need both.
