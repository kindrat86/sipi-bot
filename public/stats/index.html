Enterprise Adoption

79%
Organizations with AI agent adoption
As of 2025, 79% of organizations report some level of agentic AI adoption, with 96% planning to expand their AI agent usage. Only 4% of surveyed organizations have no plans to adopt AI agents.
Source: Landbase, 39 Agentic AI Statistics Every GTM Leader Should Know
APA: Landbase. (2026). 39 Agentic AI Statistics. https://www.landbase.com/blog/agentic-ai-statistics
88%
Regular AI use in at least one business function
88% of organizations report regular AI use in at least one business function, up from 72% in 2024. IT operations (67%), marketing (53%), and customer service (48%) lead in agent deployment.
Source: McKinsey, The State of AI: Global Survey 2025
APA: McKinsey. (2025). The State of AI: Global Survey 2025. https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/quantumblack/our-insights/the-state-of-ai
