Per-Transaction Limit

How per-transaction spending limits protect your budget.

A per-transaction limit is the maximum dollar amount an AI agent can spend on a single transaction. Set to $50, the agent can never authorize a charge above $50 — regardless of what it's prompted to do. Combined with daily caps and velocity limits, per-transaction limits are the first line of defense against accidental overspend by autonomous agents.
