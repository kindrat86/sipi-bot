What LLMonitor does well

LLMonitor is an open-source LLM observability and cost monitoring platform. It traces requests, tracks token usage per user/model/key, and provides dashboards and alerts for cost anomalies. It supports most major LLM providers and has a generous free tier.

Where it falls short for agent spend

LLMonitor is passive monitoring — it logs what happened but doesn't prevent what could happen. Its "alerts" fire after a cost threshold is breached, not before. An agent in a runaway loop will trigger an alert — after it's already spent the money. sipi.bot is active control: every spend attempt runs through the rules engine and is APPROVED, BLOCKED, or FLAGGED before the charge occurs.

When to pick each

Use LLMonitor for dashboards and retrospective analysis. Use sipi.bot for the pre-spend guardrail — the 5ms decision that prevents a $12k loop from ever starting. The two are complementary, not competitive.
