The data

Why these benchmarks matter

If you don't know where you stand, you can't tell whether your agent spend is normal or pathological. These benchmarks give you the comparison.

How to use these benchmarks

  1. Measure your current spend per task, per agent, per day
  2. Compare to the benchmarks above
  3. If you are above the 75th percentile, install SpendFirewall to enforce ceilings
