Why this matters

Understanding the pre-spend vs reactive distinction is the single most important concept in AI agent spend control. Every existing tool that touches AI spend — provider billing caps, LLM gateway budgets, observability dashboards — counts dollars after a call completes. That is a post-mortem, not a guardrail. A pre-spend firewall evaluates before the money moves. The difference is categorical: one tells you what happened; the other stops what should never happen. This is the principle sipi.bot was built on.
